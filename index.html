Common Plant Diseases
සාමාන්‍ය දළු රෝග

Fungal Diseases
දිලීර රෝග

Causes: High humidity, wet leaves.

Remedies: Fungicides, neem oil.

Bacterial Diseases
බැක්ටීරියා රෝග

Causes: Contaminated tools.

Remedies: Copper sprays.

Viral Diseases
වෛරස් රෝග

Causes: Pests like aphids.

Remedies: Remove infected plants.
